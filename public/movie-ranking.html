Movies Worth Watching

Keith's Edition

Good Will Hunting

This is a story about a troubled young man who has an amazing ability for solving mathmatical equations. He is lost in his life but finds himself through the help of a therapist with an unique way of connecting with the young boy along with finding the love of his life which guides him toward a brighter future.

Indiana Jones and the Temple of Doom

An archaeologist by the name of Indiana Jones embarks on a quest to find a magical Sankara stone, and uncovers an ancient evil which threatens all who come into contact with it.

Spirited Away

An anime with beautiful artwork about a young girl who stumbles upon an abandoned amusement park where her parents get changed into pigs and she must find a way to save them.

Limitless

A pill that allows the consumer 100% of his brain's full capacity changes a struggling writer into a finacial wizard, but also puts him in danger.

Ronin

A band of samurai set out to avenge the death and dishonor of thier master at the hands of a ruthless Shogun.

Ip Man

This is based on the true story of Ip Man who was a Chinese Grandmaster in the martial arts of Wing Chun and later became the teacher of Bruce Lee. This takes place during the Japanese invasion of China.
